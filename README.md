# COP3701-Banking-Loan-Approval-Risk-Database
Banking Loan Approval &amp; Risk Database

The purpose of this database is to ensure that the correct people are getting loans rather than those who would default on the loans or fail to repay the bank.

I would get informaiton through applications to sign up for the bank and monitoring the account's spending habits and the amount saved.

The users would be members of the bank who require loans whether they are falling on rough times, need an expensive purchase such as a car or home, or those wishing to start a business.
